Why Professionals Choose TaxPilot

Why should I use TaxPilot instead of just Googling?

Google gives you millions of results, many outdated or from unverified blogs. TaxPilot gives you **one precise answer**, extracted directly from the official 2025 government tax code, with a link to verify it. It saves you 90% of your research time.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Gemini?

Generic AI models are trained on the "whole internet," leading to hallucinations (inventing facts). **TaxPilot is restricted.** It can only answer if it finds a citation in our verified legal database. If the law isn't there, it won't guess. Plus, our interface is built for workflows—with integrated Notes and Reminders.

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption. Unlike free tools, we explicitly **do not train our models** on your private client data or uploaded documents.
